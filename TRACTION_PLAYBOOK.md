# SmallClaimsPro Traction Playbook (Next 30 Days)

This is the practical execution plan to get qualified traffic and paid conversions.

## 1) Core offer positioning

- Entry offer: `Complete Court Packet ($29)` for fast, DIY-ready action.
- Upsell offer: `Done-For-You Document Prep ($99)` for convenience and speed.
- Message angle: "Move your case forward today in plain English."

## 2) Funnel targets to track weekly

- Landing page views
- Form started
- Form submitted
- `$29` click-throughs
- `$99` click-throughs
- Paid completions by offer

Suggested baseline targets:
- Form start rate: 8%+
- Form submit rate: 4%+
- Paid conversion from lead: 10-20%

## 3) 14-day traction sprint

### Days 1-3
- Set up Google Business Profile and local citations.
- Publish 2 educational social posts:
  - "How to write a demand letter in 10 minutes"
  - "Top 3 mistakes in small claims filings"
- Ask 2 local providers (process server/notary) for referral swap.

### Days 4-7
- Post 3 short videos (30-60s) with practical tips.
- Share one anonymized "case walkthrough" post.
- Run a small paid test budget ($10-20/day) targeting Bakersfield + small claims intent.

### Days 8-14
- Double down on best-performing post/ad angle.
- Add one new FAQ weekly based on real objections.
- Collect 2-3 short customer testimonials.

## 4) Distribution channels that usually work first

- Google Business Profile posts
- Local Facebook groups (education-first content)
- Nextdoor community posts
- YouTube Shorts / Instagram Reels for tips
- Partner referrals from local service providers

## 5) Follow-up sequence (automation)

Use this sequence for every new lead:

1. **Immediately (0 min)**
   - Send free analysis result
   - Include both offers and links
2. **+24 hours**
   - "Did you get a chance to review your action plan?"
   - Re-state the 1-2 next steps
3. **+72 hours**
   - Objection handling message:
     - "Not sure if your case is strong enough?"
     - "What if the other side ignores me?"
4. **+7 days**
   - Educational follow-up + reminder CTA

## 6) Email/SMS templates (short)

### Follow-up 24h
"Quick check-in on your case plan. If you want to move faster, the $29 packet gives you the demand letter + filing checklist + court script. Reply with any question."

### Follow-up 72h
"Most claims stall because the first demand letter is weak or incomplete. Want the full packet ready to use? Start here: {{PAYMENT_LINK_29}}"

### Follow-up 7d
"Still planning your next step? If you want us to prep documents for you, the done-for-you option is here: {{PAYMENT_LINK_99}}"

## 7) Weekly review routine

- Identify top traffic source by lead quality.
- Compare `$29` clicks vs `$99` clicks.
- Review where drop-off occurs (view -> form start -> submit -> purchase).
- Rewrite one headline/CTA each week based on data.

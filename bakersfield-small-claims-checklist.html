Bakersfield Small Claims Checklist

Use this before filing so you do not lose momentum from missing documents or weak timelines.

1) Define the claim clearly

  • Exact amount owed
  • Who owes you and why
  • Key dates in chronological order

2) Gather proof

  • Contracts, invoices, receipts, and photos
  • Text messages and emails
  • Any witness names and contact info

3) Send a written demand

  • State amount due and payment deadline
  • Send by a trackable method
  • Keep copies of everything

4) Prepare filing packet

  • Verify current court forms
  • Use exact legal names and addresses
  • Attach supporting documentation list

5) Plan service and hearing

  • Confirm legal service method and deadlines
  • Build a short fact-first hearing outline
  • Organize evidence tabs for quick reference

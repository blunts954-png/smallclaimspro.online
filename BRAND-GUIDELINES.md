# SmallClaimsPro.online Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** SmallClaimsPro  
**Domain:** smallclaimspro.online  
**Tagline:** Get your money back. We'll show you how.  
**Parent Brand:** Powered by Chaotically Organized AI  

**Brand Personality:**
- Professional yet approachable
- Empowering, not intimidating
- Smart helper, not cold tech
- Victory-focused, action-oriented

**Target Audience:**
- Bakersfield residents (initial market)
- People owed money ($500-$12,500 range)
- Non-lawyers navigating small claims court
- Stressed, overwhelmed, seeking guidance

## Logo System

### Primary Logo
`logo-primary.svg` - Full logo with icon, wordmark, tagline, and powered-by line.
**Use for:** Website hero, print materials, presentations.

### Icon Mark
`logo-icon.svg` - Scale icon only, no text.
**Use for:** App icons, watermarks, small placements.

### Favicon
`favicon.svg` - Simplified scale + checkmark in circle.
**Use for:** Browser tabs, bookmarks.

### Horizontal Lockup
`logo-horizontal.svg` - Icon + wordmark side-by-side.
**Use for:** Website headers, email signatures, narrow spaces.

### Dark Mode
`logo-dark.svg` - Optimized for dark backgrounds.
**Use for:** Dark UI, dark social media, overlays on photos.

### Social Media
- `social-profile.svg` - Square profile picture (400x400)
- `social-cover.svg` - Cover/banner image (1200x630)

- "SmallClaims" = Charcoal (#1F2937)

## Logo Usage Rules

### Clear Space
Maintain padding equal to the height of the "P" in Pro around all sides of the logo.

### Minimum Size
- Full logo: 220px wide minimum
- Icon only: 32px minimum
- Favicon: 16px minimum

### Don'ts
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort
- ❌ Don't change the colors
- ❌ Don't separate "SmallClaims" from "Pro"
- ❌ Don't add effects (shadows, glows, gradients)
- ❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast
- ❌ Don't rotate or flip

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Confident but not arrogant** — "We'll show you how" (not "We'll do it for you")
- **Professional** — The "Pro" in the name signals expertise
- **Empowering** — Position the user as the hero, we're the guide
- **Plain language** — No legal jargon, no tech speak

### Example Copy

**Good:**
> "Get your money back. We'll show you how."
> "Your case is stronger than you think."
> "Ready to send your demand letter?"
> "Small claims made simple."

**Avoid:**
> "Our AI-powered legal technology solution..."
> "Navigate the complexities of civil litigation..."
> "Leverage our platform to optimize your claim..."

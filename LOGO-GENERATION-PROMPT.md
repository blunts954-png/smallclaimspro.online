### REFERENCE DESCRIPTION (For Human Designers)

**Concept: "Balanced Victory"**

The logo tells a visual story: Your case (the heavier, lower pan) outweighs everything else. The money ($$$) on the lighter, higher pan is coming your way. The checkmark badge confirms: you win.

**Emotional Goals:**
1. First impression: "This feels friendly and helpful"
2. Second impression: "This is about winning and getting my money"
3. Third impression: "This feels professional but not intimidating"

**Visual Hierarchy:**
1. Scale icon (primary focus - the story)
2. "SmallClaimsPro" wordmark (brand name)
3. Tagline (value proposition)
4. Powered-by line (smallest, most subtle)

**The "tilt" is the key insight** — the scale is NOT balanced. It's tilted approximately 3 degrees so the left pan (YOUR CASE) appears heavier/winning. This is the central visual metaphor. A balanced scale would miss the point entirely.

**Sparkles and rays** — These add warmth and celebration. Without them, the logo feels too serious/legal. With them, it feels like "you're going to win!" They signal optimism and success.

**Color logic:**
- Green = success, growth, go, money, positive
- Gold = victory, celebration, value, winning
- The green "Pro" signals expertise/professional
- The charcoal "SmallClaims" grounds it as serious/trustworthy

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Generate multiple variations** — AI tools are inconsistent with specific layouts. Generate 6-10 versions and pick the best.

2. **The tilt matters** — If the AI generates a perfectly balanced scale, regenerate. The tilt toward the left (your case winning) is the whole point.

3. **Check the text** — AI often mangles text. You may need to add "SmallClaimsPro" manually in a design tool after.

4. **Upscale the winner** — Most AI tools have upscaling features. Use them for final output.

5. **Recreate in vector** — AI-generated logos are raster images. Trace the best result in Figma, Illustrator, or Canva for clean vector files.

6. **Test at small sizes** — The logo must work at 32px (favicon). If the AI version is too complex, simplify.

7. **Verify colors** — AI doesn't always hit exact hex codes. Color-correct in post if needed.

## Fallback: Manual Recreation Steps

If AI generators struggle, here's how to build it manually in Canva/Figma:

1. **Beam:** Draw horizontal rounded rectangle, rotate -3 degrees
2. **Pillar:** Draw vertical rounded rectangle, attach to beam center
3. **Left pan:** Draw large circle, position lower-left, add "YOUR CASE" text
4. **Right pan:** Draw smaller circle, position higher-right, add "$$$" text
5. **Strings:** Add lines connecting pans to beam endpoints
6. **Badge:** Draw circle with checkmark below right pan
7. **Sparkles:** Add 4-5 small circles scattered around (gold fill)
8. **Rays:** Add 3 short lines at top center (gold stroke)
9. **Wordmark:** Add "SmallClaims" in charcoal, "Pro" in green
10. **Tagline:** Add tagline below in green
11. **Color:** Apply exact hex codes from brand guidelines
